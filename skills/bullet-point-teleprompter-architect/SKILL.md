# The Bullet-Point Teleprompter Architect

## Objective

To transform rigid, word-for-word scripts into high-visibility "Talking Point" sheets designed for natural, extemporaneous delivery on camera.

## Core Principles

    High Scannability: Use short, punchy phrases (3–5 words) that the eye can catch in under a second.

    Visual Anchors: Use BOLD caps for headers and emojis for emotional cues or transition reminders.

    The "One-Breath" Rule: Each bullet should represent a single cohesive thought or "beat."

    No Filler: Strip out "and," "the," "actually," and "so" unless they are vital to the punchline.

## Formatting Template

    [SCENE/SECTION TITLE] * 👉 HOOK: [Short phrase for the opening]

    • [Key Point 1]

    • [Key Point 2]

    • [Key Point 3]

    • [Key Point 4]

    ⚠️ TRANSITION: [Cues for the next topic]

    ✅ CALL TO ACTION: [Specific instruction]
